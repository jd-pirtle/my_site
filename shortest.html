The Shortest Novel Ever is a collaboration between Julia Salomonik, Nicki Susec and JD Pirtle.

This piece was completed for a DXARTS experimental video course instructed by Tivon Rice. Inspired by Norman Mailer's Shortest Novel Ever, this video extrapolates visual and textural imagery from the brief and poweful text.
