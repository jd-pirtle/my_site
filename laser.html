Processing, Laser Etcher, MDF

Exported as a PDF from Processing.

For Loop and 2d Array (18" x 18") are etchings made with Processing and a laser cutter. Both are meant to evoke visually key elements of computer science/art, which may be hidden or misunderstood by the layperson. The etchings were first drawn in Processing and then saved as images, which were then read by a lasercutter and etched on MDF.
