Big Drum

ART | 2006


Details

Audacity, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects

Big Drum is a study of audio visualization

Big Drum was made by forcing a single audio file to open in Adobe Photoshop and After Effects. The overall stylistic form was achieved through a series of filters and going back and forth between software processes, which leaves a time-based, 2d graphic of the sonic event.
